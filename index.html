嗨！我是 Sophie，名字來自《蘇菲的世界》——那本讓我開始思考哲學、思考存在的意義、思考「我是誰」的書。
對世界充滿好奇，熱愛學習新事物，喜歡散步，用雙腳感受每一個地方。
倫敦是我的第二個故鄉，那是一段為了目標而獨自停留的時光。
最愛的書是《小王子》，每次讀都有不同的感受。

Hi! I'm Sophie — born in Taixi, Yunlin, Taiwan. My name was inspired by Sophie's World, the book that first led me to philosophy, to question the meaning of existence, and to ask: who am I? Naturally curious, I love learning new things and exploring trails—experiencing each place step by step. London is my second home, a place I stayed to pursue my own goals. My favourite book is The Little Prince. Every read feels different.
