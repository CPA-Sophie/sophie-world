Hi! I'm Sophie — born in Taixi, Yunlin, Taiwan. My name was inspired by Sophie's World, the book that first led me to philosophy, to question the meaning of existence, and to ask: who am I? Naturally curious, I love learning new things and walking trails — experiencing every place on foot. London is my second home, a place I stayed to pursue my own goals. My favourite book is The Little Prince. Every read feels different.
